WHO WE SERVE

Industries We Empower

AK Robotic delivers precision automation, welding intelligence, and smart manufacturing solutions across five key industrial verticals — each tailored to the unique demands of the sector.

Automotive Industry

We deliver end-to-end robotic welding cells, jig & fixture systems, and PLC-controlled assembly lines purpose-built for Tier-1 and Tier-2 automotive suppliers. Our solutions handle body-in-white welding, chassis sub-assembly, and powertrain component production — achieving weld consistency above 99.7% and throughput gains of 30–50%.

  • Robotic Welding Cells
  • BIW Assembly Lines
  • PLC Automation
  • Vision Quality Inspection
01

Fabrication & Welding Industry

From heavy structural steel fabrication to precision sheet metal processing, AK Robotic integrates advanced MIG, TIG, and spot welding automation systems. We design custom welding fixtures, deploy seam-tracking robots, and supply top-tier welding equipment from brands like Fronius, KEMPPI, and EWM — reducing rework by up to 85% and maximising arc-on time.

  • MIG / TIG / Spot Welding
  • Custom Welding Fixtures
  • Seam Tracking Robots
  • Equipment Supply & AMC
02

Manufacturing Units

General and discrete manufacturing units rely on AK Robotic for complete automation upgrades — including cobot integration, special purpose machines (SPMs), offline robot simulation, and industrial panel board solutions. We map your production workflow and implement scalable, modular systems that boost OEE by 20–40% while minimising changeover time.

  • Cobot Integration
  • Special Purpose Machines
  • Offline Simulation
  • OEE Optimisation
03

Packaging Industry

For FMCG, pharma, and consumer goods packaging lines, we engineer high-speed pick-and-place robotic systems, automated palletising cells, and conveyor-integrated quality-check stations. AK Robotic's solutions operate at production rates exceeding 120 packs/minute with sub-millimetre placement accuracy and seamless integration into existing ERP/SCADA ecosystems.

  • Pick & Place Robots
  • Palletising Automation
  • Conveyor Integration
  • SCADA Connectivity
04

Small & Medium Enterprises (SMEs)

AK Robotic firmly believes automation should not be exclusive to large corporations. We offer right-sized, budget-conscious automation solutions specifically designed for SMEs looking to modernise operations without prohibitive capital expenditure. From entry-level cobot deployments and PLC-based machine control to robot training programmes and Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC), we grow alongside your business — ensuring technology remains your competitive advantage, not your constraint.

  • Entry-Level Automation
  • AMC & Support Contracts
  • Robot Training
  • Scalable Solutions
  • Cost-Optimised ROI
